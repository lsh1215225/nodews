Director Changes

Joss Whedon directed the first two Avengers films, known for his witty dialogue and character interactions.

The Russo Brothers (Anthony & Joe Russo) took over the latter two films, bringing a more epic and emotional storytelling style.

Marvel Cinematic Universe

The Avengers series forms the core of Phase Three of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, known as the "Infinity Saga," perfectly concluding the storylines of 22 films.
